Welcome to the Grand Display of Star Trek Artifacts, where the wonder of interstellar exploration meets the allure of the silver screen. Immerse yourself in the authentic costumes and props that graced the iconic sets of each Star Trek series.

Star Trek: Voyager

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the Delta Quadrant with our Star Trek: Voyager exhibition. This meticulously curated collection showcases a wide array of costumes, weapons, and props that played an integral role in the captivating adventures of Captain Janeway and her intrepid crew. Stand in awe of the detailed Starfleet uniforms, the Borg tech-infused costumes, or the intricate alien attire, each piece echoing the unique narratives woven into this series.

Feel the weight of a replicated phaser or marvel at a model of a tricorder, and immerse yourself in the tangible remnants of Voyager's historic 75,000-light-year trek. From encounters with the Kazon to alliances with the Borg, the exhibition encapsulates Voyager's spirit of survival, exploration, and the enduring pursuit of home. Whether you're a die-hard Trekkie or a budding Star Trek enthusiast, this exhibition offers a unique portal into the heart of Voyager's rich tapestry.

Star Trek: Deep Space Nine

Step aboard our Star Trek: Deep Space Nine exhibition, an exceptional journey into a diverse, complex, and riveting corner of the Star Trek universe. Celebrated for its multi-dimensional characters and groundbreaking story arcs, Deep Space Nine continues to resonate with fans and first-time viewers alike. This exhibition shines a spotlight on the series' captivating array of costumes, weapons, and props, each element reflecting the depth of the cultures that converged at this iconic space station.

Stand close to an authentic Bajoran robe, hold a model of a Jem'Hadar disruptor, or explore the array of Ferengi items, an homage to their unique entrepreneurial spirit. Our exhibit illuminates the complex tapestry of life on the frontier of explored space, encompassing the drama and the dynamics of this groundbreaking series. Whether you're a longstanding fan or a newcomer, this exhibition provides a rich and tangible connection to the diverse and nuanced world of Deep Space Nine.

Star Trek: The Next Generation

Welcome to our Star Trek: The Next Generation exhibition, a journey into the 24th century and the voyages of the starship Enterprise-D. With its compelling stories, iconic characters, and profound exploration of moral and societal issues, The Next Generation continues to captivate audiences, embodying the very spirit of Gene Roddenberry's vision. This exhibition offers an immersive experience into the show's universe with a collection of original costumes, weapons, and props that played pivotal roles in the series.

Come face to face with the elaborate Starfleet uniforms, Klingon bat'leths, or an android's positronic brain. Witness the evolution of the series' visionary design, from the sleek tricorders to the formidable phaser rifles. This is your chance to relive the memorable moments of The Next Generation, to encounter the tangible pieces of the series that resonated far beyond the final frontier. Regardless of whether you're a lifelong Star Trek enthusiast or have yet to make your first trek, this exhibition offers a captivating look into the heart of this beloved series.
